🎓 Key Concepts

Osmosis: Water moves across membrane toward higher solute concentration.

Semi-Permeable: Membrane allows water through but blocks solutes.

Water Potential: Water moves from high to low water potential.

Equilibrium: Equal water potential on both sides.
